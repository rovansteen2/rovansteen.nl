Emil Ruder

Parallax website

As part of a school assignment I had to create a website about typography and a influentical person. I created a website about Emil Ruder, a Swiss typographer and graphic designer whom was known for the Swiss Style: sans-serif typefaces, page grids for structure and symmetrical layouts. I tried to make the website a special experience by creating lots of parallax effects and animations. It took me some time to finish but it taught me a lot about Javascript, jQuery and parallax effects.
